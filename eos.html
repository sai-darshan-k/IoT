Key Findings:

  • Production costs can be reduced by 30% at scale (>250 units), significantly improving profitability.
  • The most efficient segment appears to be Medium Farmers (4.94-9.88 acres) with balanced acquisition costs and strong margins.
  • Fixed costs like soil testing tools (INR 7,000) are effectively amortized when serving more than 25 farmers.
  • Break-even is achieved between 11-25 units where 10% cost reduction helps reach 0% profit margin (neutral point).
  • Higher volume production (>50 units) allows for competitive pricing while maintaining healthy profit margins.

Note: Fixed costs amortization shows how initial investment costs are spread across more units as production volume increases.
